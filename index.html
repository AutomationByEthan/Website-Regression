What We Offer: UI Automation

Selenium-Based Automation Suite

Leverage our robust Selenium framework for:
- User Acceptance Testing (UAT): Ensure your software meets end-user needs with precise validations (boolean, calculations, loops).
- Regression Testing: Verify functionality across Chrome, Firefox, and Edge with comprehensive test suites.

Detailed Reporting

Gain actionable insights with:
- Client-Reviewed Screenshots: Visual proof in CSV reports for transparent test results.
- Data-Driven Validations: In-depth CSV reports with pass/fail metrics, tailored to your business needs.
- Cumulative Suite Report: Quick analysis to streamline decision-making.

Adapt our solutions to your needs with:
